Acharya Sudarshan Patna Central School Patna, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day Cum Boarding School, with classes from Nursery to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in Patna. Acharya Sudarshan Patna Central School was established in 1980. It is a Trust and is part of Acharya Sudarshan Patna central school and is managed by . Acharya Sudarshan Patna Central School is a Coed Day Cum Boarding School, which services the educational need of over NA students from Nursery through XII. The classes in Acharya Sudarshan Patna Central School for every academic year starts from April to March The School got affiliated to CBSE upto +2 level in 1993.
